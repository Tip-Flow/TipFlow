CRA COMPLIANCE

Stop worrying about tip taxation. TipFlow has it covered.

Most Canadian restaurants are getting tip classification wrong. TipFlow makes sure you're not one of them.

Direct Tips 💵

Customer tips go straight to staff

No CPP or EI deductions required. Staff self-report on their T1 personal tax return. TipFlow automatically notifies staff of their CRA obligation at every payout.

Most common in Canadian restaurants

Controlled Tips 🏦

Tips pooled and distributed by management

Treated as employment income. CPP and EI deductions apply. TipFlow flags every controlled tip payout so your payroll provider knows exactly what to process.

Common in large restaurant groups

Your complete CRA audit trail — automatically
