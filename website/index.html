Process

How Mise works

From shift end to bank account in minutes, not days.

1

Import shift data

Connect your POS — Square, Lightspeed, or CSV. Mise pulls sales and hours automatically at shift end.

2

Tips calculated instantly

Role weights, hours worked, and custom rules — Mise computes every allocation in seconds. Zero errors.

3

Manager confirms

One tap to review and approve. Biometric auth required. Full audit trail created automatically.

4

Staff paid directly

EFT hits staff bank accounts same night. No cash. No waiting. Staff see it in their Mise app immediately.
